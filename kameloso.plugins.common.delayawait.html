kameloso.plugins.common.delayawait

kameloso plugins common

The section of kameloso.plugins.common that deals with delaying executing of core.thread.fiber.Fibers and delegates to a later point in time, and registering such to await a specific type of dialect.defs.IRCEvent.

This was all in one plugins/common.d file that just grew too big.
