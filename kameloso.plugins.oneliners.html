kameloso.plugins.oneliners

kameloso plugins

The Oneliners plugin serves to provide custom commands, like !vods, !youtube, and any other static-reply !command (provided a prefix of "!").

More advanced commands that do more than just repeat the preset lines of text will have to be written separately.
