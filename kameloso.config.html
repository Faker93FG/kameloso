kameloso.config

kameloso

Functionality related to configuration; verifying it, correcting it, reading it from/writing it to disk, and parsing it from command-line arguments.

Employs the standard std.getopt to read arguments from the command line to construct and populate instances of the structs needed for the bot to function, like dialect.defs.IRCClient, dialect.defs.IRCServer, kameloso.kameloso.IRCBot and kameloso.kameloso.CoreSettings.
