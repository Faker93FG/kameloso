colourWith

kameloso terminal

Takes a mix of a TerminalForeground, a TerminalBackground, a TerminalFormat and/or a TerminalReset and composes them into a format code token.

This is the composing overload that fills its result into an output range.

Examples

Appender!(char[]) sink;
sink.colourWith(TerminalForeground.red, TerminalFormat.bold);
sink.put("Foo");
sink.colourWith(TerminalForeground.default_, TerminalReset.bold);
