initLogger

kameloso common

Initialises the kameloso.logger.KamelosoLogger logger for use in this thread.

It needs to be separately instantiated per thread, and even so there may be race conditions. Plugins are encouraged to use kameloso.thread.ThreadMessages to log to screen from other threads.
